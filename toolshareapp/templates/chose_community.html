Changing a community in ToolShare

You are in {{user.community}}

Changing a community will move all your tools to a new community, cancel all future reservations on any of your tools (and alert the borrowers), and sends a message to any borrower that currently has a tool of yours. You may need to coordinate with any current borrowers of your tools.

A community is for sharing tools within a certain zone. Choose a community that fits your location so that you can share tools easily between your house or within nearby sheds.

If you want to create a new community make sure it is a large enough zone that people want to join it. Good community zones are neighborhoods, schools, and workplaces.
